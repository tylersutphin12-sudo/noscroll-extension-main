NoScroll Privacy Policy

NoScroll does not collect, store, or sell personal data.

  • All data (time saved, blocked count, settings) is stored locally in the browser.
  • No browsing history is tracked beyond what is needed for functionality.
  • No personal data is sent to external servers. Font files may be loaded from Google Fonts; no personal data is sent in these requests.
  • NoScroll does not process payments or collect payment information at this time.
  • The extension only uses permissions required to function (modifying pages like YouTube, Instagram, etc.)
